In a village devoid of young people, a carousel transformed into a rejuvenation machine upsets the balance of the surviving elderly, leading them to discover that death is not the end.

directed by Ottavia Farchi  
written by Ottavia Farchi  
photography by Nicolò Colaianni  
edited by Ottavia Farchi, Nina Bonatti  
live recording by Marco Candela  
sound design by Sabino Schingaro  
original music by Loraaxis (Edoardo Partipilo) and Sabino Schingaro  
1AD Adriano Guario
